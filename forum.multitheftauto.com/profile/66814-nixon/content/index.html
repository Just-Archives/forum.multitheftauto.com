            22. PC fans loud even low temps

              Nixon replied to PresKoYT's question in Client

              When was the last time that you cleaned the heatsink grills inside your PC for dust? Your fans may be working extremely hard because there is so much dust. I had the same problem with my notebook PC and my fans were very loud. Then after some weeks, the fans completed stopped working. I had to order new fans from China.... .. Dont let this happen to you. CLean your heatsink grill today! Ps: Remember to disconnect the battery of your notebook PC if u start opening it and touching it. And only use dry cotton swabs to clean the grill.
