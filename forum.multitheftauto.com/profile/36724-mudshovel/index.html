            5. [G&T] Survival island Beta V1.1 3DSMax models (Update)

              Mudshovel posted a topic in Showroom

              Hello all! G&T Mapping here again, as usual at your service! Providing you with the latest high quality maps FOR FREE, made by us. Introduction: We're happy to announce that we're ready to take the next step with our survival island build, and share it with the community for further testing and enhancing the build, that said this build is intended for developers and enthousiast that want to be part of creating something bigger than just a map. Ofcourse we can't do this ourself, we need the community's help, ideas and feedback. Map description: At the moment the map layout is relatively simple with a few rivers, mountains, and grass areas. All the terain, surfaces and mountains are made in 3Ds Max by G&T. To spice things up we've include two custom skyscrapers aswell. Direct link to skyscraper resource here: https://community.multitheftauto.com/index.php?p=resources&s=details&id=10763 https://community.multitheftauto.com/index.php?p=resources&s=details&id=10762 We've designed them so you can grab them and move them around and in theory create your own world with just these few objects. Screenshots: This map will come with: *Custom models. *Custom textures. *Custom scripts. Our Goals: First of all what it comes down to is, we want to create a custom made world map that works independently from the standard GTA:SA world, a open sandbox style map that consist out of custom models giving the users the tools to add their own sections to the world. We would also very much like a custom dedicated game mode to include with the map. What do you expect from us?: From developers, we would very much apreciate to add their own game modes and share these with community, they dont have to consist out of alot of complex coding, even just a few small dedicated game modes will work fine to take this to the next lvl. From enthousiast all we ask is that you give us much needed feedback, we need you to let us know what you like or hate about it. Also hit us up on Social media to show your support! How to install: *The world will be a separate map file including some resources, and all the textures. *Simly unpack the zip folder in your server's resource folder. *Turn on gtloader through the console or admin panel to auto-load all the models, and you're good to go. *If you want to run this map next to your exsisting free roam map you will have to load up both .map files. *Open this map with 'play' gamemode then type: /tpme to teleport yourself to the island.. (We encourage testers to use their own game modes) Breakdown of things to add: *Dedicated Game mode. *Multiple islands. *Desert area. *Suburban area. *Bridges. Download link: http://www.mediafire.com/download/dglsq ... l-life.zip Feedback. All the feedback, suggestions and bug reports regarding any of our map packs may be submited on these forums or our social media. Please be clear about what kind of bug you're submiting and when you encountered it. As always we will keep you posted on new changes, we've got a few nice things in store for you! ''soon'' G&T Mapping Needs You! Do you have any experience with recording videos in MTA, Mapping, Moddeling, or Scripting? feel free to send us a message so you too can join the G&T Mapping team! Thanks!
