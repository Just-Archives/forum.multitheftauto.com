              SpoonTheMan replied to Ren_712's topic in Resources

              Enb palettes are uncompressed bitmabs (.bmp). The shader panel supports ENB palettes -but you have to compress them to (.png). Open them in raster graphics editor (like gimp, photoshop etc.) and save as .png (try to maintain best quality as possible). You can then replace existing palettes with yours.
