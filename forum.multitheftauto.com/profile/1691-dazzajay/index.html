            4. [HELP]Replace car paintjobs?

              DazzaJay replied to irinel1996's topic in Scripting

              Replacing the paintjobs does work, subenji99 did it. HOWEVER the paintjob id's are NOT the vehicle ID's. You need to use the new replacing shader functions. for the life of me i dont know how its done, (benji did it) but i do know our textures are just .png images. For example "elegy2.txd" as its called in the gta3.img, with just the texture extracted to .png is: elegy2body256.png then you replace "elegy2body256" with that somehow in shaders (someone who knows will be able to help you with that) and TADA! custom paintjob. Doing it this way ONLY replaces the SINGLE paintjob. Not all 3.
