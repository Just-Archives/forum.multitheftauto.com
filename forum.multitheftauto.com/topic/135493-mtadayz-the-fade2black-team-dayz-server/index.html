            The Fade2Black Team ( DayZ )
            Hello, I'm here with a New DayZ Mod, I'm Eren, I've been working on MTA DayZ Scripts, and I've been working on my own server for 1 year.


            To introduce myself, I'm Eren, I've been playing MTA for years and I'm enjoying it, I have dual citizenship (I'm Turkish and German) I'm new to script development and I'm a beginner.
             


            Before moving on to the server's features, I would like to make a few remarks,We are trying to open the server in a better quality as a result of really long efforts, I really recommend this server for those who like to play MTA DayZ, please review,Our server is currently in BETA version, we will release it as a FULL Version in a few weeks.
             


            DISCORD ADDRESS : discord.me/fade2black / https://discord.gg/DeK2yS44PM  You can reach the MTA DayZ IP address via Discord.

            TeamSpeak3  ADDRESS : If you want, you can log in with a numeric IP address or you can log in by typing the following. (80.253.246.8  / fade2black or f2b)  [all versions can login]

            features of our server :
             

            • VIP House ( vip members will create their own vip house and can hide their loot indoors. )
            • VIP features ( VIP members will be able to use their own skins when spawned,VIP members will be able to create motorbikes in exchange for Shop Tokens,When VIP members spawn, some loot is given to them by typing ./viploot ,VIP members can paint their cars with a single command,VIP members are open to the use of the Hunt system, so they can play more enjoyable games with other players...)
            • Chase Mission (Think of a car, you have to protect this car from enemies within certain minutes, if the car is under your control, you will be rewarded when the time is up.)
            • Interiors (you have access to the interiors of some buildings, for example police stations and casinos,there are better loot indoors.)
            • 2x Zombie Area (There are powerful zombies waiting for you in the inner area of the driving school in the SF area,These zombies that are very hard to kill are full of loot,You can easily collect Level/Shop Tokens in this area...)
            • Ranks (Colors assigned to ranks so you can distinguish everyone.)
            • Skills (Skills given to weapons such as the M4 AK, thus allowing for smoother gameplay.)
            • Missions (Big hack in the big ear,The mission to hijack the Rustler plane,war mission on secret islands,Mission to hijack police car in police station.)
            • War Room (Area51) (In the secret room in area 51, you can find the traces of serial killers. (5 or more players killing players). )
            • Garages (You can buy a garage to store your vehicles,You can use the ./rentgarage command to rent a garage.)
            • CCTV (CCTV in secret areas)
            • Level System 
            • War System (Red areas are formed in some selected areas, if you fight in these red areas, Shop Tokens are given to the 1st, 2nd and 3rd players.)
            •  AND MORE.

            the server is now active, you can login
            server IP address : mtasa://91.134.166.76:22013 

            hello dear Fade2Black community as you know we are updating the VIP house system because there are multiple bugs,For now, we rewrote and finished the system, you won't have a panel like the old VIP House system, we've made it easier and more practical now, you just have to learn some codes.

To purchase a VIP membership, you must contact the Founder. (Our VIP membership discount is valid for the first 3 people for now),we are trying to make the prices the lowest, now let's go to explain the system.

First, choose a place in the outside world for the entrance to your home. The only rule of thumb with house positioning is that entry must be through a door or window (or something else that can be used as an entrance) that is not currently going anywhere, and cannot be made in Area 69 or any other interior space. . Once you've chosen your entry location, stand by the door (not too close or too far - it's best to be about half a meter away from the door) and enter /createhouse in the local text chat (press T).

Now your home has been created. As with any other interior, you can get in by standing at the door and pressing F. People who don't have a key to the house won't be able to see the opened door message, so you don't have to worry about people finding your house and camping outside unless you take them straight home.

you can give your house key to another player if you use ./tkey [ID] command you can give a temporary key to the player, if you want to give permanent key to the player you can use ./pkey [ID] command. (if you want to get the key back from the player you should use the ./delkey [ID] command)

You can set a message to be displayed to people when they enter your house with /hmotd message. For example. Welcome Shady's VIP House Unlimited. (if you buy more than one house you must name your houses. [Welcome Shady's #2 VIP House Unlimited & Welcome Shady's #3 VIP House Unlimited.])

If, for some reason, you want to completely remake your house from scratch, or you don't want a house anymore, you can destroy it and all of its contents by saying /destroyhouse.

we are giving you the 1st house for free, if you want to increase the number of your house you should buy it with Shop Token
2. House (500 Shop Token) / 3. House (600 Shop Token)

That's all the details of the system for now, we will make new updates and announcements in more innovations.

Fade2Black Staff & Developer.
