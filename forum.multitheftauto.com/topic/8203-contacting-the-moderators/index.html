            NOTE: This is an archived post, and information in here no longer applies to the forums. Everything in this section is kept only for nostalgia!

            TL;DR = Don't try to useÂ this

            The mods are clearly understaffed right now and people need to come to them, not the other way around. This is where you say "But wait, I contact mods and they never respond!!!". Well, usually the problem is you are only contacting ONE moderator. Now you can contact ALL moderators easily!

            Click the [ Contact the moderators ] button at the top right (next to profile, personal messages, login/logout) to contact the moderators. You can also click here:

            CONTACT THE MODERATORS

            Enter your subject and message. This will create a message in a forum called "Complaints" that ONLY moderators can view. That means you will not be able to view the board or your submitted message.

            If this is not working for you, you can try the old fashioned method, sending a private message to 1 or more mods:

            I had a rummage through this area and found lots of individual topics about templates people had made. I would not make an impractical template with a page generator like most of them did, I actually write web pages by hand to Strict XHTML standard, linked with a fully validated CSS2 file. The style would be like the GTAForums skin I am making, in that I would make a completely stripped skin for the boards and then re-build everything from scratch manually.

            The basic principles I create websites and skins by are:-

            1. Ease of use is the most important aspect.
            2. Non-standard colours make websites harder to use.
            3. Conventional interfacing makes pages easier to use.
            4. Fluid (resizable) layouts cater for all desktop sizes.
            5. People just use websites to get information, so the simpler the information is presented the easier time people will have.
            6. Efficient use of code makes speedy download times.

            I won't hurry you all into making a decision. IJS said to me over MSN that he wants to discuss it with you all first, so I'll wait until I hear how that has turned out.
