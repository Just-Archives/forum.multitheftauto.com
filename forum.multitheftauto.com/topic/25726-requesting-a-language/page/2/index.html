            We create these boards on a case-by-case basis so there is no exact amount for it. This is because there are other important factors when we want to introduce a new language subforum:

            - the amount of existing forum users from said country or who are using said language

            - general popularity of MTA in said countries

            - the existence of potential moderators and forum leaders for such sections
