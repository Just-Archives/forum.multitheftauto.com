            The link to the video: http://www.fileplanet.com/dl.aspx?/plan ... ribute.wmv

            The counterpart to Vice City Tribute has been released. Liberty City Tribute is a unique and original video that pays homage to Grand Theft Auto III. Video of New York City is mixed with comparison shots from GTA III to show the similarities between the real city and the game's version. This is followed by a tribute to the game itself.

            This video combines the two videos I mentioned a few weeks back. It turns out that they are better when combined. Instead of having "GTA III Tribute" and "The Real Liberty City" (based off The Real Los Santos) separate, I discovered that Liberty City Tribute made the video a whole lot better.

            Two months of thought and work went into the video, and several people need to be thanked. First off, thanks to ashdexx who created a tool that allowed me to take the GTA III HUD completely off the screen. Secondly, thanks to Racer_S for his Vice City Camera Hack tool that I used a lot for the video. Third, thanks to the GTA:Liberty City team since I used it combined with the cam hack tool. Lastly, thanks to DigitalD and ElBazo for their input on early versions of the video. The rest of the credits are in the video. Look for additional comments by me in the news comment post. Other websites do have permission to post the video, but please let me know if you do post it somewhere else here: [email protected]

            From http://www.planetgrandtheftauto.com
