            Finally i got these weapon pickups working. This is one out of many, many new features in this upcoming Update.
            You can decide if you only want to pick up the ammo (if it fits to your weapon!) or if you want to switch the weapon completely (and pick up the ammo), but you will lose the ammo that is currently in your clip.

            In order to avoid ammunition/pickup spam, these pickups will be deleted once the player who dropped them respawns. This also means that you cannot suicide and pick up your own dropped weapon again after respawning.
            Stay tuned. Its getting warm. Really warm.
