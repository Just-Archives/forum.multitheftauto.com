            Allright, IJsVogel asked me to make this game mode, so I better start working on it =). For now, I have plans on making the Game Mode like this:

            - One Team (or player) drives the bus freely around Liberty City, and has to keep the speed above 40 for a certain period of time ( 2 mins?)

            - The other Team drives Rumpo's or Pony's and have to stop the bus.

            - The bus is bulletproof, explosionproof, fireproof and has quite some amount of hitpoints.

            - The bus is made heavy with some platinum bars, like in the mission 'Bullion Run', therefore will be harder to stop it.

            - The team driving the Rumpo's will have the ability to make roadblocks at certain points which will be visible on the radar for the player driving the bus.

            Any other ideas or suggestions will be taken in to consideration.
